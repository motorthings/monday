Stage 2 — Intake Flow

Stark Industries PMO · form submission → triage routing

LIVE · forms.monday.com

The public-facing intake form captures 8 fields from any Stark Industries employee. On submission, an item lands on the Intake Board. In Stage 5, an AI Summary column will read the Business Problem field, and a Completeness Formula will drive routing logic. In Stage 6, Connect Boards will link approved items to the Portfolio Board.
